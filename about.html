About the Game

SPECTRE QUEST: Eternal Ascent is a roguelike adventure where every run is unique. Ascend through dangerous biomes, battle relentless enemies, and grow stronger with each attempt as you climb toward the unknown.

Combat System

Experience a dynamic turn-based combat system inspired by action-heavy timing mechanics. Drawing influence from titles like Infinity Blade and Mario RPG, combat blends strategy with player input — rewarding precision, timing, and smart decision-making.

Items & Progression

Discover over 100 unique items that shape your playstyle each run. Wield one of 20 distinct weapons, each with its own strengths, and experiment with a wide variety of powerful spells.

Deep Customization

Build your character through a complex system featuring over 100 passives, buffs, debuffs, and status effects. Adapt your strategy on the fly and create powerful synergies to overcome increasingly difficult challenges.
