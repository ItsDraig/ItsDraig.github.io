Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms will the game be available for?

The game will be available on Windows and Mac at launch, with planned ports for console and mobile in the future.

Where can I purchase the game?

The game will be available on Steam for Windows and Mac. For now, you can support development through our Kickstarter.

I want to play the game

You can play the original demo or sign up to become a playtester for the latest builds. Links can be found through our social media pages or Kickstarter.

Will the game be hard?

The standard difficulty is designed to be very challenging, staying true to the roguelike experience. However, a more casual difficulty will be available for players who prefer a more relaxed experience.
